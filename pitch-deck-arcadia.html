The Problem

Managed trading today is built on assumption, not proof.

Trader Fraud

Anyone can spin up a fund, attract investor money with a pitch, and blow it up day one. Investor capital is unprotected until track record exists.

Broken Trust Model

Investors ask: "Will this trader act responsibly?" But they have no way to enforce it. Risk depends entirely on the trader's goodwill.

Access Gap

Serious investors have capital but no way to deploy it without trusting a stranger or trading themselves. Distribution is broken.

Custody Risk

Traditional managed products require you to hand your money to a third party. Solana offers an alternative: non-custodial proof.

2

The Solution: Arcadia

A protocol that makes trust verifiable, risk enforceable, and capital accessible.

Mandatory Paper Mode

Every vault runs 30 days with trader capital only. On-chain track record is public and immutable before a single investor dollar enters.

Protocol-Enforced Risk

Position limits, mandatory 20% junior reserves, loss waterfalls—all automated. The code enforces it. The trader can't breach it.

Instant Liquidity

Investors withdraw anytime. No queue. No permission. The protocol calculates share of every vault asset and transfers instantly.

Non-Custodial Rails

Traders execute on Jupiter. Investors self-custody. Settlement is on-chain. Nobody touches the money except the protocol.

3

How Arcadia Works

Step 1: Trader Deposits

Trader posts first-loss capital ($5K-$20K). Vault enters 30-day paper mode. Only trader capital at risk.

Step 2: Build Track Record

30 days of on-chain trading via Jupiter. Every trade is public, auditable, permanent. No editing. No story.

Step 3: Graduate

After positive performance, vault graduates. Investors can now deposit. Trader junior buffer protects them.

Step 4: Earn Fees

Trader earns fees only on gains above high-water mark. No fees during drawdowns. Skin in game stays aligned.

4

Why This Requires Blockchain

These three properties can only exist on-chain:

  • Verifiable Track Record — Every trade is on-chain forever. No company can edit it. No one can lie about performance.
  • Permissionless Access — A trader in Lagos can raise from an investor in Singapore. No bank account needed. No country restrictions.
  • Automatic Enforcement — Loss waterfalls, position limits, and risk controls happen at the protocol layer. The code is stronger than promises.
  • Instant Settlement — Investors can withdraw anytime. No centralized party can freeze or delay. Atomic settlement on-chain.

Revenue Model

Arcadia takes 10% of trader fees. We earn only when traders succeed.

Trader Economics

  • Earn: 15-20% performance fee on gains above high-water mark
  • Keep: 90% of fees (Arcadia takes 10%)
  • At risk: Junior buffer (first-loss capital)
  • No downside fee: Zero fees during drawdowns or flat performance
