# Arcadia Colosseum Pitch Research Notes

## Source Discipline

This deck intentionally avoids unverifiable external market numbers. It uses:

- Product facts visible in the Arcadia app and repo.
- Current protocol design choices already implemented or documented in the project: paper mode, first-loss junior capital, USDC/WSOL custody, Pyth-backed NAV, reserve guard, and Jupiter-assisted unwind design.
- Public ecosystem nouns only: Solana, Pyth, Jupiter, USDC, WSOL.

## Numbers Policy

- No TVL, market-size, user-count, revenue, or competitor metrics are claimed.
- Any future external statistic must be sourced on the day of submission and added here with URL, date, and retrieval note.
- Unknown traction remains framed as build progress, not invented momentum.

## Judge Framing

The pitch is optimized for Colosseum/Solana judges: technical clarity, demo completeness, and why the product needs Solana-native settlement and public records.
