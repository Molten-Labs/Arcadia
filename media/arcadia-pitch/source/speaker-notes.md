# Arcadia Colosseum Pitch Speaker Notes

## 1. Arcadia makes capital follow proof

Open with the incentive problem. Managed vaults are attractive because good traders can compound capital, but the trust model is broken. Arcadia says: before a trader earns investor allocation, they prove themselves with their own capital first.

## 2. Managed DeFi vaults still run on trust

Explain the three failures plainly: managers can market before proving, they often carry too little downside, and investors can get stuck waiting for manual liquidity decisions.

## 3. Solana rails make proof-gated vaults practical

The point is not “we use Solana.” The point is that low-latency settlement, public state, Pyth valuation, and Jupiter routing make the safety model enforceable inside the product.

## 4. A marketplace for traders who prove first

Show the marketplace as the investor’s first screen. It should feel like discovery with risk context: buffer, health, strategy, capital, track record.

## 5. The trader’s money is the first fuse

The junior buffer is the heart of Arcadia. Losses hit the trader’s capital first. As the buffer weakens, the protocol tightens the trader’s operating room.

## 6. One flow, two roles, strict boundaries

Judges should notice that investors and traders do not share the same interface. Investors see marketplace, portfolio, and claims. Traders see manager controls, vault config, and trading operations.

## 7. Withdrawal safety is part of accounting, not support

This is the serious protocol slide. Investors deposit and withdraw USDC. Traders can hold WSOL exposure, but NAV uses fresh prices and withdrawals can require an unwind before payout.

## 8. Arcadia is stronger because it is on-chain

If we removed Solana, the product would lose the public proof layer, non-custodial settlement, and composable performance records. That is the crypto necessity.

## 9. The MVP is already a real product surface

Keep this honest. Do not pretend there is production TVL. Show that the app, flows, tests, and accounting design are ready for pilot pressure.

## 10. Help Arcadia recruit the first serious managers

The ask is specific to Colosseum: help us find disciplined pilot traders, review the protocol, and move from a polished MVP to guarded live capital.
