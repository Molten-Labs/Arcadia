# Anti-Concepts

What Arcadia is explicitly NOT.

## Arcadia Is NOT Copy Trading

Copy trading replicates a trader's positions into follower wallets. Arcadia is a managed capital structure — investors allocate capital into a vault, the Trader manages it inside the vault. The Trader does not execute trades in the investor's wallet.

## Arcadia Is NOT Pooled Custody

Capital in an Arcadia vault is held in PDA-controlled Treasury accounts. No single keypair controls the pool. The Trader cannot withdraw investor funds.

## Arcadia Is NOT an Opaque Hedge Fund

Every vault's NAV history, graduation status, fee claims, cooldown events, and freeze history are visible on-chain. Positions are private during live operation but become fully public after vault close or freeze.

## Arcadia Is NOT Social Trading

There are no follower counts, no signal feeds, no chat rooms, no copy buttons. The social layer is reputation — a cryptographically verifiable track record, not engagement metrics.

## Arcadia Is NOT a Lending Protocol

Senior Investors earn a target floor rate from trading profits, not from borrower interest. Idle USDC in the vault treasury is just USDC — it is not routed to any lending platform.

## Arcadia Is NOT a Prediction Market or Betting Platform

Vaults execute real trades (spot swaps via Jupiter). No binary outcome, no resolution oracle, no event contract.

## Arcadia Is NOT a DAO-Governed Protocol (At Launch)

The 0.2% withdrawal fee rate, cooldown thresholds, freeze trigger, position limit bands, and liquidity floor are protocol constants. The protocol has a multisig for emergency pause and program upgrades. Governance is a future roadmap item.
