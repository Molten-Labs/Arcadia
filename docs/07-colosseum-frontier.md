## CRITICAL CHANGE: No Tracks

Frontier **removed all official tracks/categories**. No Consumer, DeFi, Infrastructure, or **Mobile Award** tracks. All projects compete in one pool.

This means:
- No "Mobile Award" safety net (Breakout had a $25K Mobile Award — LootGo won it)
- TapTribe must stand out on overall merit: business model, demo quality, team
- Being mobile-native still differentiates from the majority of web-only submissions

### Side Tracks (Bonus)
Side tracks on **Superteam Earn** from regional communities (Superteam Georgia, Ukraine, etc.) offer up to $10K. Worth exploring for additional prizes.

## What Makes Winners Stand Out (From Past Hackathons)

### Patterns from Cypherpunk & Breakout Winners

1. **Repeat builders** — Unruggable (Grand Champion) appeared in 3 hackathons before winning
2. **Full-time commitment** — judges want companies, not experiments
3. **The 3-minute video is king** — judges cite this as the #1 differentiator
4. **Viable business model** — revenue plan in the first 30 seconds
5. **Team of 3+** — solo builders rarely win; winning teams average 3+ members
6. **AI + Payments** is hot (Latinum, MCPay both won)
7. **Privacy** is a recurring winning theme (Vanish, Cloak, Unruggable)

### Past Winners Reference

**Breakout (Apr-May 2025):**
- Grand Champion: TapeDrive (decentralized storage, $50K)
- Consumer: Trepa (sentiment prediction mobile app)
- Mobile Award: LootGo (mobile rewards app, $25K)

**Cypherpunk (Sep-Oct 2025):**
- Grand Champion: Unruggable (hardware wallet + companion app)
- Consumer: Capitola (prediction markets aggregator)
- DeFi: Yumi Finance (on-chain BNPL)

## TapTribe-Specific Strategy

### Strengths to Emphasize
1. **Physical demo moment** — NFC phone tap is visceral and memorable
2. **Clear business model** — event badges ($50-500/event), premium profiles ($2/mo)
3. **Seeker ecosystem** — 1.2M+ devices, dApp Store distribution
4. **Soulbound badges** — "can't be faked, can't be transferred" (strong for judges)
5. **No competition** — 0 past winners in NFC social identity niche

### Weaknesses to Address
1. **Solo builder** — winning teams average 3+ members. Consider finding co-builders.
2. **No dedicated mobile track** — must compete on overall merit
3. **User traction** — get beta testers before submission. Even 10-20 users helps.

### Pitch Video Priorities
1. Open with the problem (networking at crypto events = chaos)
2. **Show the tap** (close-up, two phones, dramatic) — this is the "aha moment"
3. Business model within first 30 seconds
4. Show soulbound badges, on-chain data in Explorer
5. Revenue: "$100K ARR from event badges alone"
6. Close with vision: "the social layer for Solana Mobile"
